Thank you for your interest in contributing to the **Technology of Expressions (TE) / Ordinative Sciences Framework**.

## Guiding Principles

Before submitting an issue or a Pull Request, please ensure your contribution adheres to the **Axioms of TE Core**:
1. **No Form Without Content**: Do not add filler text, vague guidelines, or buzzwords. Every word must hold structural weight.
2. **Beware the "Projective Void"**: Framework rules must be precise. Leaving ambiguous loop-holes to accommodate multiple incompatible truths is considered manipulative (Active Projective Void) and will be rejected.
3. **Coherence over Consensus**: Contributions will not be accepted simply because "many people agree" or because of "statistical truth." Structural evidence and applicability across the Arajat logograms are required.

- **Isomorphism Check**: Ensure that the foundational principles (STEER, SHACK, ERES, AA, GLIO) remain exactly the same, merely translated into the specific domain syntax.

If you find a contradiction, a semantic vulnerability, or a case where the Router logic could lead to *Attractor Lock*:
- Open an Issue titled `[TE_CORE ALIGNMENT]` or `[TE_BOOTLOADER LOGIC FIX]`.
